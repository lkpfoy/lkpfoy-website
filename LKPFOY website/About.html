Lekoa Presbytery - Fellowship of Youth

Welcome to the Lekoa Presbytery Fellowship of Youth. A small Presbytery that consists of 7 FOY branches within the Presbytery. We are a community of believers committed to worshipping God, growing spiritually, and serving our community.

The LKPFOY is a ministry that strives to empower young people to bear witness to the Gospel of Jesus Christ through word and actions. It is one that aligns with the Vision, Mission and Aims prescribed by the UPFOY, which includes promoting Christian values, leadership development and active participation in church life.

We aim to achieve a space where we learn / teach one another, support one another and generally play a positive role in improving and developing one another in both spiritual and personal lives.
